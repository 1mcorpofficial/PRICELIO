# Special features (moat)

## In-store barcode scan
- Instant price sanity vs real paid history.
- Show better options nearby.

## ShelfSnap
- Photo of shelf label becomes fresh proof.
- Reputation, geo bounds, and anomaly filters protect against abuse.

## Package size trap detector
- Highlight cheaper unit price alternatives.

## Project baskets
- Templates for repair, baby, pets, etc.
- Optimized across DIY and specialty stores.

## Receipt vault
- Track warranty-relevant items and reminders.
- Optional and not required for MVP.
