# Summary

## Goal
Build a mobile-first price and promo intelligence app that compares real paid prices with official flyers and online offers, then produces a simple saving plan.

## Core value
- After purchase: scan a receipt and see overpaid items and better options next time.
- Before purchase: build a basket and get the cheapest plan with 1-2 stores.
- Map: see nearby stores with top deals and proof level.
- Categories: grocery, DIY, beauty, pharmacy.

## Why it is hard to copy
- Data moat: receipts + flyer history + price time series + confidence model.
- Quality control: product matching, anomaly filters, verified status.
- Map + basket algorithms with travel cost tradeoffs.
