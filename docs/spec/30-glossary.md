# Glossary

- Flyer (Advertised): official store offer from flyer/catalog.
- Online offer: e-shop price page or feed.
- Real paid: price paid on a receipt.
- Confidence: likelihood data is correct.
- Verified: offer confirmed by receipts or independent proof.
- Disputed: sources conflict and must be shown clearly.
- Deal Score: summary score for value and proof.
- Ingest: automated import/extraction of source data.
- Matching: map raw items to canonical products.
- Travel cost: time/distance cost used in optimization.
