# Map and city system

## Launch strategy
- Seed cities: Vilnius, Kaunas, Klaipeda, Siauliai.
- New cities appear once data threshold is reached.
- Low-data cities show advertised only and mark low verification.

## Store pin content
- Top 3 deals by savings with toggle to percentage.
- Verified share for that store.
- Freshness timestamp.

## Map filters
- Category filters.
- Verified-only / advertised-only / mixed.
- Distance radius.
- Sort by savings, percent, or popularity.

## Store card
- Top deals today and ending soon.
- Personal categories (opt-in).
- Verified stats window.
- Add-to-basket action.

## City feed
- Deal of the day, price shocks, new flyers, top category picks.
