# Anti-fraud and abuse prevention

## Threats
- Mass upload spam.
- Fake receipts or edited images.
- Fake shelf labels.
- Click farm to inflate deal trends.

## Protections
- Rate limits + device/IP limits + captchas on risk.
- Reputation weighting for confirmations.
- Anomaly detection vs median.
- Cross-check shelf data with receipts/flyers and mark disputed.
- Trend filters to reduce single-user abuse.
