# Personalization and alerts (opt-in only)

## What we personalize
- Rank frequently purchased items higher.
- One-tap regular basket suggestions.
- Cheaper alternatives when preferred items are expensive.

## Alert types
- Price threshold.
- Category deals.
- Basket price drop.
- Ending soon.

## Channels
- PWA push when available.
- Email at start.
- In-app notifications.

## Privacy controls
- Opt-in/opt-out anytime.
- Delete history and personalization profile.
- Explain what data is used.
